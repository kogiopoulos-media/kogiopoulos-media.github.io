Services / Wedding & Events

The moments
between
the moments.

We photograph weddings the way they feel — raw, warm, and utterly alive.

"The best wedding photos aren't posed. They're found."

We approach every wedding as observers first. Our role isn't to direct your day — it's to witness it with extraordinary attention. The glance across the room before you walk down the aisle. The way your mother squeezes your hand. The chaos and laughter of the dance floor at midnight.

These are the photographs you'll still be looking at in fifty years. Not the staged ones — the real ones. Kogiopoulos Media brings a documentary sensibility to wedding photography, blended with artistic vision and technical mastery.

We are available for weddings across Australia, Greece, and internationally. Each wedding is photographed by a lead photographer, with second-shooter options available for larger events.
